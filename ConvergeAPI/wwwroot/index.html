USPTO Filing Process

With a standard USPTO fee, your mark will be filed under your name. USPTO would assist with document completion and filing with USPTO on your behalf. Upon filing, you will be provided with a filing receipt along with a serial number which further can be used to track the status of your application. Once the filing is through, the USPTO takes around 3 – 5 months to assign the application to an examining attorney, who then manually reviews the application and decides on the approval. The overall Trademark registration process may take up to 9 – 10 months to get approved.
